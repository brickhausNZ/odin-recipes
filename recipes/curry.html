Thai Green Curry

A simple and creamy winter warmer

Required ingredients

  • Chicken breast (500g)
  • Coconut cream (300mL)
  • Green curry paste (3 Tsp)
  • Brocolli (500g)
  • Spinach (100g)
  • Onion (One Whole)

Instructions

  1. Heat a large pan on high and add a splash of olive oil
  2. Chop onion finely and add to pan along with the curry paste, cook until onions are browned
  3. Dice chicken and add to the pan along with 100mL of the coconut oil
  4. Once chicken has begun browning, add the rest of the coconut cream and brocolli
  5. Lower the heat of the pan to a simmer and cook for 10 minutes
  6. Remove the pan from the heat and then stir through the spinach to soften it
