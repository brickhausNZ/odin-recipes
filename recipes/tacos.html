Tacos

A fun dinner for the whole family

Required ingredients

  • Beef mince (500g)
  • Tomatoes (200g)
  • Lettuce (100g)
  • Carrots (100g)
  • Cheese (100g)
  • Onion (One Whole)
  • Spice mix (One pack)
  • Hard taco shells (8)

Instructions

  1. Heat a large pan on medium heat and add a splash of olive oil
  2. Chop onion finely and add to pan, cook until onions are browned
  3. Add the beef mince to the pan along with the spice mix
  4. While the mince is cooking, grate cheese and slice vegetables to your liking
  5. Remove the mince from the heat when brown and leave to rest for a few minutes
  6. Stack your hard taco shell with the mince, cheese and veges
  7. Enjoy! :)
