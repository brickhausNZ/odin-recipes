Scrambled eggs

The quickest and tastiest breakfast

Required ingredients

  • Eggs (4)
  • Butter (30g)
  • Cream (30mL)
  • Salt & Pepper (To taste)

Instructions

  1. Heat a large pan on low and add the butter, stir until it begins to melt
  2. Crack the eggs into the pan and immediately begin whisking them into the butter
  3. While whisking, add the cream slowly about a spoonful at a time
  4. Stir the eggs gently until the mixture begins to become firm
  5. Remove the pan from the heat and let the residual heat in the pan finish the eggs
  6. Serve and enjoy! :)
